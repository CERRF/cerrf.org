Privacy Policy

Our commitment to protecting your personal information and privacy.

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how CERRF.org (the “Site”) collects, uses, and discloses your personal information when you visit our website. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled securely and responsibly.

Information We Collect

We may collect personal data such as your name, email address, and other contact details when you interact with our website (for example, via contact forms or newsletter subscriptions). Non-personal data may also be collected through cookies and analytics tools.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to provide and improve our services, update you on our initiatives, and respond to your inquiries. We take steps to ensure your data is secure and only used in accordance with this policy.

Information Sharing

We do not sell or trade your personal information. Occasionally, we may share your data with trusted third-party providers who assist with operating our website or servicing our users. These parties are obligated to keep your information confidential.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or request deletion of your personal data. For any privacy-related inquiries or requests, please contact us at contact@cerrf.org.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Any significant changes will be posted on this page along with an updated effective date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at contact@cerrf.org.

About CERRF.org

The Cyber Education, Resilience and Research Foundation (CERRF) is a youth-led nonprofit dedicated to cybersecurity education, research, and digital resilience. We empower the next generation with essential skills and innovative learning experiences.

Our Mission

Empowering youth through innovative cybersecurity education and fostering a secure digital future.
